Volunteer Today

Explore rewarding volunteer opportunities at our horse and donkey rescue organization! Contribute by feeding, grooming, and exercising animals, or get involved in farm maintenance, fundraising, and administrative tasks. Connect with our dedicated team to find out more.

Feeding Tasks

Ensure equines have constant access to clean and fresh water.

Forage (hay or pasture) is the primary component of the diet. Take the animal for a stroll so they can graze.

Grooming Tasks

Learn how to carefully wipe around the eyes with a clean, damp cloth to remove discharge.

Enjoy a soothing experience for both you and the horse and donkey by using a body brush to remove dirt, debris, and loose hair from the coat. Learn how to use a curry comb in circular motions to loosen dirt and stimulate the skin.

Hooves require daily cleaning. Clean hooves using a hoof pick to remove dirt, rocks, and debris. We will teach you how to inspect the hooves for signs of injury, infection, or abnormalities.

Exercising Tasks

Once you get comfortable with the animals, we will teach you different methods of providing physicaal and mental exercise.
