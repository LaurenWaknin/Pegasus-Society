Your Compassion Saves Lives!

At Pegasus Society, we are dedicated to rescuing and rehabilitating horses and donkeys in need. Your support is the driving force behind our mission to provide care, love, and a second chance to these incredible animals.

How Can you help?

One-Time Donation Sponsor a Rescued Animal Volunteer Spread the Word
Make a one-time donation to support our ongoing efforts in rescuing and rehabilitating horses and donkeys. Your contribution ensures these majestic creatures receive the care they deserve. Consider sponsoring a specific rescued horse or donkey. Your sponsorship directly contributes to their healing journey, providing essential resources for their rehabilitation. Become a vital part of our mission by volunteering your time and skills. Learn more about our volunteer opportunities and actively contribute to the rehabilitation of these beautiful animals. Help us raise awareness by sharing our mission on social media. Follow us on Facebook and share our stories to amplify the impact of your support.
